using the driver:

1) Turn on AR.Drone

2) Connect to the drone WiFi with your computer

3) roslaunch ardrone_driver ardrone_driver

4) For teleoperation, roslaunch ardrone_driver ardrone_wiimote_teleop.launch

5)Enjoy! 

NOTE*

You may have some trouble connecting your drone via WiFi to machines running Ubuntu. If you are unable to join, the best thing to try is a restart of nm-applet.
